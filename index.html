OVERVIEW

JFM Digital LLC ("we," "us," or "our") builds LEO Pay Tracker and MedPay Tracker — professional pay tracking apps for iPhone. This privacy policy applies to both applications.

We built these apps with a simple principle: your personal and financial data belongs to you, and only you. We have no servers, no user accounts, and no visibility into anything you enter.

NO CLOUD STORAGE
NO ACCOUNTS
NO ANALYTICS
NO ADS
NO DATA SHARING
NO THIRD-PARTY SDKS

DATA COLLECTION

We collect nothing. All data you enter — salary, schedule, overtime logs, leave balances, expenses, off-duty job records, officer profile, and pay period configuration — is stored exclusively on your iPhone using iOS local storage (AsyncStorage).

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it using the built-in export features.

EXPORTS & GENERATED DOCUMENTS

The apps include features to export your data as files (HTML summary, CSV spreadsheet) and to generate PDF documents (OT Slip / Overtime Voucher). These files are created entirely on your device.

Sharing those files — via email, AirDrop, Messages, or any other method — is entirely your choice and your action. We have no visibility into what you share, with whom, or when.

OT Slip / Overtime Voucher Notice: The overtime slip feature generates a PDF document for personal recordkeeping purposes only. It is not an official payroll or department document. All calculations are based solely on data you entered into the app. Verify all amounts with your payroll department before submission or reliance on any generated document.

BACKUP & RESTORE

The Backup & Restore feature creates a JSON file on your device containing your app data. You control where this file is saved or shared. We never receive, store, or have access to backup files.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our apps do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. There are no third-party SDKs that transmit your data anywhere.

The apps use Apple's standard iOS frameworks for local storage, file system access, and document sharing — all of which operate on-device.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our apps are designed for working professionals and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
