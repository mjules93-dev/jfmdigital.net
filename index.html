ABOUT

From the docks to the badge

I built LEO Pay Tracker because I couldn’t find an app that correctly calculated my hourly rate on a Pitman schedule. Every existing tool assumed 2080 annual hours — wrong for most law enforcement schedules and leading to inaccurate OT pay previews.

MedPay Tracker came next. Healthcare workers face the same problem — complex differential pay, rotating 12-hour shifts, and paychecks that are nearly impossible to verify manually.

I’m a patrol officer with the New Haven Police Department. My family ran Juliano Fish Market in New Haven for decades. JFM Digital is named in their honor.

Both apps are free. Feedback from officers and nurses across the country drives every feature.

— Officer Michael Juliano · New Haven PD · JFM Digital LLC
