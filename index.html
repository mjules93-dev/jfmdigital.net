FEATURES

Everything you need to track your pay

💰

ACCURATE HOURLY RATE

Calculates your real hourly rate using your actual scheduled hours per pay period — not the standard 2080 hours that undercounts officers on Pitman, Panama, or non-standard schedules.

🔶

OVERTIME TRACKING

Log road jobs, detail work, and mandatory OT at 1.5×, 2×, or any custom rate. Supports higher rank pay — enter annual salary or a direct hourly rate.

📅

SCHEDULE BUILDER

Supports 4/2, Pitman, Panama, DuPont, and custom alternating weekly schedules. Day, evening, and night shift color-coding on your calendar.

📊

YTD EARNINGS

See your year-to-date base salary, overtime, and training pay at a glance. Tracks multiple contracts — salary changes mid-year are handled automatically.

🏖

LEAVE BALANCES

Track vacation, sick, comp time, and custom leave banks in days or hours. Automatic carryover and reset date support.

📤

SHARE YOUR SCHEDULE

Export your shift calendar as a .ics file and share it to iOS Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook. Choose 1, 3, 6, or 12 months.

PRIVACY

Your data never leaves your phone

LEO Pay Tracker stores everything — your salary, schedule, OT logs, and leave balances — exclusively on your device using iOS local storage.

There are no servers, no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics. We have no visibility into how you use the app or what data you enter.

ABOUT

Built by a New Haven officer

I built LEO Pay Tracker because I couldn't find an app that correctly calculated my hourly rate on a Pitman schedule. Every existing tool assumed 2080 annual hours — which is wrong for most LEO schedules and leads to inaccurate OT pay previews.

I'm a patrol officer with the New Haven Police Department. My family ran Juliano Fish Market in New Haven for decades. JFM Digital is named in their honor.

The app is free. Feedback from officers has driven every feature added since launch. If something is wrong or missing for your department, reach out.

— Officer Michael Juliano · New Haven PD · JFM Digital LLC
