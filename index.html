FEATURES

Everything your union contract demands, tracked precisely

01

ACCURATE HOURLY RATE

Calculates using your actual scheduled hours per period — not the standard 2080 that undercounts Pitman, Panama, and non-standard schedules.

02

OVERTIME TRACKING

Log road jobs, details, and mandatory OT at 1.5×, 2×, or any rate. Supports higher rank pay by annual salary or direct hourly entry.

03

SCHEDULE BUILDER

Supports 4/2, Pitman, Panama, DuPont, alternating weekly patterns, and custom rotations. Day, evening, and night shift color-coding.

04

YTD EARNINGS

Base salary, overtime, and training pay tracked year-to-date. Multiple contracts handled automatically when salary changes mid-year.

05

LEAVE BALANCES

Vacation, sick, comp time, and custom leave banks tracked in hours or days. Automatic carryover and reset date support.

06

Export your shift calendar as a .ics file to iOS Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook. Choose 1, 3, 6, or 12 months forward.

ABOUT

From the docks to the badge

I built LEO Pay Tracker because I couldn’t find an app that correctly calculated my hourly rate and overtime with ease. Every existing tool assumed 2080 annual hours — wrong for most law enforcement schedules and leading to inaccurate OT pay previews.

I’m a patrol officer with the East Haven Police Department. My family ran Juliano Fish Market in New Haven for decades — a New Haven institution known to anyone who grew up in the city. JFM Digital is named in their honor.

The app is free. Feedback from officers across the country has driven every feature added since launch. If something is missing for your department, reach out.

— Officer Michael Juliano · New Haven PD · JFM Digital LLC
